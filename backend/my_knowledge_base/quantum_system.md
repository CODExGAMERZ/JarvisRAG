# Quantum System Specifications

The experimental Antigravity stabilizer system uses a core fluid consisting of highly pressurized Helium-3. The system operates at a critical temperature threshold of exactly 1.8 Millikelvin. Running the stabilizer above 2.1 Millikelvin triggers an automatic thermodynamic shutdown sequence to prevent vacuum containment leakage.

## Safety Protocols

If thermal runout is detected, standard recovery protocols require injecting liquid argon into the heat shield within 12 seconds.
